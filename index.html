    The Team

    Pixxel is a team of undergraduates from BITS Pilani, interested in reinventing space technology in India. Originating from the core team of Hyperloop India, the only Indian finalist team from among the 2500 teams that applied to the prestigious SpaceX Hyperloop Pod Competition. The team thus not only has a prior experience dealing with a project of this magnitude but also the experience of actually building a functioning Hyperloop prototype in an unprecedented two months. Pixxel has now expanded to constitute students and faculty from the top engineering and business schools of the country—namely BITS Pilani, NIT-Surathkal, IIM- Ahmedabad — consulting with space, AI and data experts from organizations like ISRO, NRSC, MapMyIndia and TRA law.
